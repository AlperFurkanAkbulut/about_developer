VOLUNTEERING PROJECT

Examining the Concept of Natural Resource Curse in the Context of the International Relations Discipline: The Example of the Eastern Mediterranean

This project was carried out in the framework of the TUBITAK 2209-A program. Within the project's scope, democracy levels, internal war and regional instabilities, diplomatic behavior patterns in the Gulf of Guinea, the South China Sea, and the Eastern Mediterranean were analyzed with qualitative and quantitative methods with data obtained from different datasets. It was aimed to understand the status quo rather than proving the existence or non-existence of the natural resource curse. The findings obtained from these two regions were interpreted in the Eastern Mediterranean.
